GCS - Global Citation Score
shows the Citation Frequency based on the total count in the Web of Science.

LCR - Local Cited References
shows the count of cited papers within the collection.

LCS - Local Citation Score
shows the Citation Frequency within the collection.

CR - Cited References
shows the number of all cited references as given in the paper's bibliography.

Recs - Number of Records
shows the number of records where a given item is found.

T* - Total [score]
Any Total score represents a sum of respected scores for all records from a given author, source, other category, or all records.
The following scores are available in Bibliometric view.
Percent
Ratio of the item's frequency to the number of all records.

NA - Number of Authors

LCS/t - Local Citation Score per year
shows the average LCS per year since the publication date.

GCS/t - Global Citation Score per year
shows the average GCS per year since the publication date.

LCSb - Local Citation Score in the beginning
shows the LCS only over the period since the beggining of the collection time span until an arbitrary cutoff year.

LCSe - Local Citation Score in the end
shows the LCS only over the period since an arbitrary cutoff year until and including the last year of the collection time span.
